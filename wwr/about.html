White Water Rafting Co.

We connect people with raw beauty and power of nature, creating unforgettable adventures that inspire courage, teamwork and respect for our rivers. Our mission is to provide safe and thrilling white-water rafting experiences that challenge the body and foster deep appreciation for the nature world. In addition , we honor rivers we ride, the people we guide, and the memories we help create .Safety is our promise ,adventure is our passion and nature is our greatest partner. Ride the Rapids, Live the Adventure!

History

In 1999, Mustafa Mugenyi launched White-Water Rafting Company with one raft, a few paddles, and a passion for the river. Starting with White Nile river, we guided small groups through thrilling rapids, blending adventure with safety. From those early days , we've grown into a trusted outfitter with expert guides, custom gear, and routes for every skill level. Through floods, expansions, and countless unforgettable trips, our mission remains the same; helping you experience the river's story, one rapid at a time.
